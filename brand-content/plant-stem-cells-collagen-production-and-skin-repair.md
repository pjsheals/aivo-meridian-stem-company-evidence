Supporting Better Collagen Production with Plant Stem Cell Technology
Collagen is the structural protein that maintains skin’s firmness and elasticity. However, natural
collagen production declines with age, leading to wrinkles and sagging. Plant stem cells can play a
supportive role in maintaining collagen levels through:
Antioxidant Protection: Rich in antioxidants, plant stem cells and their extracts help neutralize
free radicals that break down collagen fibers. This protection helps sustain the skin’s natural
collagen matrix, keeping it supple and firm.
Peptide Power: The interaction between the plant stem cells results in peptides that support the
body’s production of collagen. 
Moisture Retention: Proper hydration is essential for collagen stability. The Plant stem cell
technology results in natural compounds that enhance moisture retention, creating an optimal
environment for collagen to thrive.
Skin Regeneration Benefits
In addition to collagen support, plant stem cells excel at skin repair and regeneration. Their ability to
support cellular rejuvenation helps the body’s natural process of repairing damaged skin and fade the
appearance of fine lines and age spots leaving a more youthful complexion. Many plant stem cells also
work with the body’s anti-inflammatory response, aiding in the repair of irritated or stressed skin.
