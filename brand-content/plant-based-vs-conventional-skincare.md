What Is Conventional Skincare?
Conventional skincare products often contain synthetic ingredients, preservatives, and artificial
fragrances. While some of these ingredients are designed to enhance product performance, others—
such as parabens, sulfates, and phthalates—have been linked to skin sensitivities and environmental
concerns.
Benefits of Conventional Skincare:
Longer shelf life - Preservatives prevent bacteria growth
Targeted treatments - Designed for specific skin concerns like acne
 
Plant-Based vs. Chemical Skincare: Which One Should You Choose?
When it comes to plant-based vs chemical skincare, the best choice depends on your skin type and
personal preferences. If you have sensitive skin or prefer natural alternatives, plant-based products are
a great option. However, if you require advanced formulations for specific concerns, some conventional
products may be beneficial.
